Mole People

The concept for my final project is that I want to create a website for mole people. I want to create a fake forum in which mole people communicate and talk about their daily life. this Forum will talk aobut mole people customs as well as the food they eat. this website will also have a page where you can donate to the mole people.

The myth of mole people has been around for a long time. there are many videos on the internet talking about mole people with no actual proof. this website seeks to make it look like the mole people have existed right under our noses the whole time.
